Tennis Buddy is a tennis court reservation platform that allows a user to reserve tennis 
courts in their local area ahead of time so they don't have to worry about showing up to the court
and not being able to play tennis.

Features:
User Account Creation
User Login with existing credentials
Reservation of a court based on date and time
Ability to view assigned reservations

Roadmap/Features to add:
Ability to reserve a court within a time window that starts and expires
A forum for Tennis players to connect and schedule time to play against each other
Outbound email confirmation that sends you an email that has your court reservation, date, and time.
